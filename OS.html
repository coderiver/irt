Meet your new Command Centre for everything. From orders to guests — all parts of your business becomes accessible and manageable.
Our powerful yet simple software brings all control right into your hand.

Orders and tables

You will see and control what happens on every table. Our system collects every order and processes it to the staff for execution. You can overview the situation from one sight.

Live order map

This page will show you “eagle eye view” of your restaurant. Empty tables, served tables and waiting table are all seen on your screen. Moreover, you can change their position on screen by drag and drop to simulate room layout.

Detail table view

With detail table view you can control situation within specific table. Icons indicates order status, waiter calls and check-out requests. Your restaurant manager will not miss any action!

Point of Sales

Classical POS functionality in a modern shell. Manage orders, print bills, apply discounts with our powerful yet user-friendly POS module.

Menu Management

It is a great tool for creating “live” menu for every dish you offer. Upload picture or movie, write description, choose a price and options for discounts. Voila! Smart and engaging interactive menu sells your dishes much better than ordinary one.

Statistics

How much do you sell? How big your profit is? Who is your visitors? We can give you an answer on this questions with elegance. This window shows a clear view of how your business performs in real time.
